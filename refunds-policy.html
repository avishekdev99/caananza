Refunds Policy

Caananza (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to providing transparency regarding refunds. This Refunds Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are handled for services and bookings made through our platform. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined here.

1. General Refund Policy

Caananza operates as an intermediary connecting users with third-party hotel providers. As such, all refunds for bookings and services are subject to the terms and conditions of the respective provider. We strongly encourage users to review the provider’s cancellation and refund policies before completing a booking

2. Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:

a. Cancellations by the User:

Refunds for cancellations depend on the provider’s policy. Many providers offer refunds within specific timeframes or under certain conditions. These details will be included in your booking confirmation.

b. Cancellations by the Provider:

If a provider cancels your booking, refunds will typically be processed in full. Please refer to the provider’s policy for specific terms.

c. Errors in Booking:

If a booking error occurs due to a system issue or mistake on our part, we will work to resolve the issue and facilitate a refund where applicable.

3. Non-Refundable Bookings

Some bookings may be non-refundable, as specified by the provider. This information will be clearly stated at the time of booking. Caananza is not responsible for refunding amounts for non-refundable bookings.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1. Contact the hotel or service provider directly to confirm their refund policies.

2. If additional assistance is needed, contact us at info@caananza.com with the following details:

  • Booking reference number
  • Name and email associated with the booking
  • Reason for the refund request

We will review your request and assist in facilitating communication with the provider if required.

5. Refund Processing

Refund processing times depend on the provider’s policy and the payment method used. Typically:

  • Refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your payment method after approval by the provider.
  • If processed through Caananza, we will notify you of the refund status and expected timeframe.

6. Fees and Charges

Refunds may be subject to cancellation fees or service charges as outlined by the provider. These fees will be deducted from the total refund amount. Caananza does not charge additional fees for processing refunds.

7. Exclusions

The following situations are generally excluded from refunds:

  • No-shows or unused bookings.
  • Cancellations made outside the provider’s eligible refund period.
  • Disputes or dissatisfaction with the provider’s service unrelated to Caananza.

8. Contact Us

For questions or assistance regarding refunds, please contact us:

  • Email: info@caananza.com

Our team is here to assist you and ensure your experience with Caananza is smooth and transparent.
