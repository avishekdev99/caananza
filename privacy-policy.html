Privacy Policy

Caananza (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website, services, and tools (“Services”). By using Caananza, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from you:

a. Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact Details: When you contact us, subscribe to our newsletter, or use our contact form, we may collect your name, email address, and other details you provide.
  • Booking Details: If you book through our platform, we may collect information related to your travel plans and preferences.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We collect details such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages viewed, links clicked, and time spent on the site.

c. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and deliver relevant advertisements. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our Services.
  • Process bookings and send confirmations.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send newsletters, updates, and promotional offers (with your consent).
  • Monitor site usage and improve functionality.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activities.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following ways:

a. Service Providers: We work with third-party providers to facilitate our Services, such as payment processors, email marketing platforms, and analytics tools. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

b. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

c. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Your Rights and Choices

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access and Correction: Request access to your personal data or correct inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@caananza.com.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Data Security

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the “Effective Date” at the top will be updated. We encourage you to review this policy periodically
